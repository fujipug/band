The Salty Turtle Timeline:

The Members:

Alexander Sears

Grammy and Golden Globe nominated songwriter and producer, Alex began his guitar career in his early teens. After starting a number of Rock bands, it wasn't until he joined the Salty Turtles in 2004 that he had his first big break.

In his own words: "I never knew what the Salty Turtles had in store for me and my career, but I had a good feeling. I'm grateful to be surrounded by the amazing musicians that make up the Salty Turtles today."

Abdulaziz Alhawas

Abdulaziz, professionally known by his stage name Abdul, is a singer, songwriter, record producer, voice actor, and choreographer. Abdul started his career as a professional soccer player. He was at the same level with the likes of messi and ronaldo. unfortunately the Argentinian mafia arranged him to get an injury because he was stealing messi's spotlights. after retiring from soccer. Abdul found singing to be his new passion. He joined the Salty Turtles and the rest is to be remembered by history

Lane Fujikado

Lane began playing the bass guitar at the age of 6 months; most people considered him a baby prodigy. During his early teens he started his own band called the Salty Pugs. Unfortunately due to crack and sex addiction he fell into a downward spiral and was kicked out of the Salty Pugs. After a long road of recovery, Lane came clean of his addictions and contributed his musical talents to the Salty Turtles.

Erin Bailey

Starting out as a struggling actress trying to make it in LA right after high school, Erin truly has the most inspirational story of all the Salty Turtles. She got her big break when she found the other band members arguing over an Olive Garden breadstick when she went out for lunch one day. The rest is history. From then on, she overcame her fear of strobe lights and has been performing all over the tri-state area ever since.
